# Great AI Podcasts

> Goal: invest 2-3 hours weekly to build technical depth and market judgment.

## Must-listen list

- [Latent Space](https://www.latent.space/): practical AI engineering and tool stacks.
- [Practical AI](https://changelog.com/practicalai): real-world implementation and MLOps.
- [TWIML AI Podcast](https://twimlai.com/podcast/): broad coverage across research and industry.
- [The Cognitive Revolution](https://www.cognitiverevolution.ai/): frontier models and strategic ecosystem shifts.
- [Lex Fridman Podcast](https://lexfridman.com/podcast/): long-form conversations with researchers and builders.

## Optional additions

- [a16z Podcast](https://a16z.com/podcasts/): commercialization and investment perspective.
- [Acquired](https://www.acquired.fm/): platform strategy and company-building history.

## Listening framework

1. Listen by topic, not by release order.
2. For each episode, capture 3 conclusions, 2 debates, and 1 action item.
3. Link podcast mentions back to papers for deeper validation.

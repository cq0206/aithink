# 90-Day AI Learning Roadmap

## Days 1-30: build your input system

1. Build your source list from [X / Twitter](/en/sources/x-twitter).
2. Curate 5 high-value items daily and write short summaries.
3. Listen to 2 podcast episodes per week and capture key insights.

## Days 31-60: strengthen paper reading

1. Read 2 papers per week (start with [papers](/en/sources/papers)).
2. Publish one weekly paper comparison (method, evidence, usability).
3. Reproduce one small idea in a project (RAG, agents, or evals).

## Days 61-90: ship and publish

1. Publish one public note each week (blog or social thread).
2. Ship one demo feature every two weeks.
3. Maintain a source scorecard and remove weak inputs.

## Completion criteria

- Explain mainstream LLM system design in 10 minutes.
- Evaluate model value and risk independently.
- Convert paper ideas into working prototypes.
